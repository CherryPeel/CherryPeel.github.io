站内简介：

本站将用于“精品收集站”，初步类目将定为：

  • 动漫图片；
  • 风景图片；
  • 电影公映信息展览；
  • 文章创作；
  • 其他信息公示。

投稿

如果你也想把你的作品放在这里展示，你可以邮箱至此：ayc@ayc.moe。

  • 图片投稿请进行压缩，当然也可以选择直接投稿图片链接集合；
  • 文章请备注投稿文章类型，如小说；
  • 所有稿件还请遵守相关法律，构建和谐人类社会。

A brief introduction to the site:

This site will be as a “boutique collection station”. The preliminary category will be as follows:

  • Anime picture;
  • Landscape picture;
  • Film release information exhibition;
  • Article creation;
  • Other information be public.

Contribution.

If you also want to display your work here, you can email it here: ayc@ayc.moe.

  • Please compress the picture contribution, of course, you can also choose to submit the picture link collection directly；
  • Please note the contributed articles, such as novels.；
  • All manuscripts should also abide by the relevant laws and build a harmonious human society.
